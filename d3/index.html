Understanding the impact: A view of the city

Our analysis breaks up the city of New Orleans into its Census tracts to process financial and demographic data. However, rather than displaying by location, we organized the communities in a different way.

Here we have the central organization of the chart- you can view this as the "impact center," where damage to communities increases as you approach the middle circle.
Here is a sample of the communities. You can see communities with different statuses related to their recovery. The darker the community, the more vulnerable it was to upheaval. Some were abandoned (the striped boxes), some were gentrified (dark red), and some were vulnerable to population turnover but remained ungentrified (medium red).
These variations appear at all damage levels...
...and across multiple income levels.
